Help Center

How can we help?

If you are experiencing issues with LayarFilm, please check the common troubleshooting steps below:

  • Video not playing? Try switching to a different provider server using our built-in provider options on the movie's page.
  • App crashing? Make sure you have downloaded the latest version of the LayarFilm APK from our official website, and ensure your Android device meets the minimum requirements.
  • Subtitles missing? Check the player's CC button to manually select your preferred subtitle language. If it's unavailable, the current provider might not support it.

For further assistance, please reach out to our support team at support@layarfilm.com.
